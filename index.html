About AAA (J-POP)

AAA (pronouced as Triple A) is a Jpop co-ed vocal group created in 2005 and is currently consisting of Takahiro Nishijima, Misako Uno, Mitsuhiro Hidaka, Shuta Sueyoshi, and Shinjiro Atae.

On January 15, 2020, it was announced the group would go on hiatus at the end of the year, the members said this is no infinite hiatus and hope the fans will wait for them, the members will continue their solo activities.

More: AAA Official Website
